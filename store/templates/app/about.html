All about Online Store

Category

The online store enables you to have add category (Visible Only to the Admin) that can additionally be filled with several items under that category. It's just a simple way of trying to replicate what usually happens with eCommerce market places.

Item

Under items, you have the ability to add a particular item that fits the recommended category. You can also add a picture, a short description, your shops web address and a short description of the shop.

Registration

Under sign up or registration you can sign into the app, in order to have the ability to add items within categories. It also provides you with the ability to add your profile picture.
